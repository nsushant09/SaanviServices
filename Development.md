# Saanvi Services Website – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Overview

**Project Name:** Saanvi Services Website
**Goal:** Build a modern, minimal, emotionally engaging website that communicates care, trust, and professionalism—while prioritizing Individual Disability Support services.
**Style Direction:** Minimalism + Scrollytelling (inspired by Tilda-style storytelling experiences)
**Primary Emotion:** Warmth, care, dignity, and trust

---

## 2. Branding Guidelines

### Colors

* **Primary (Logo):** `#FF597C` (Soft Pink – represents care, compassion)
* **Secondary (Text):** `#685E5F` (Muted Brown – grounded, professional)
* **Background:** `#FFFEFE` (Near-white – clean, soft)

### Typography

#### Logo Fonts (already provided)

* Host Grotesk
* Hubbali

#### Website Fonts (choose 1 primary + 1 secondary)

* **Primary:** Jost (modern, clean, accessible)
* **Secondary:** EB Garamond (for storytelling sections)
  *Alternative:* Imprima (if cleaner tone preferred)

## 12. Technical Recommendations

### Stack:

* Frontend: Next.js / React

* Lazy load images
* Optimize animations

---

## 13. Accessibility (Important)

* High contrast text
* Readable font sizes
* Screen reader support
* Keyboard navigation
* Clear CTA buttons

---

## 14. Success Criteria

* Clear understanding of services within 5 seconds
* Emotional connection through storytelling
* Easy navigation to contact
* Mobile responsiveness
* Smooth and engaging scroll experience

---

## 15. Final Notes for Developers

* Prioritize **Individual Disability Support** visually and structurally
* Maintain **minimal design** (avoid clutter)
* Ensure **emotional storytelling flow**
* Use **consistent spacing and typography**
* Focus on **human-centered UX**
